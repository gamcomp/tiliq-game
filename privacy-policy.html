Privacy Policy for Tiliq

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tiliq ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Tiliq mobile application ("App").

1. Account Sign-In

To save your progress across devices and enable leaderboards and friends, the App requires you to sign in with Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple. When you sign in, we receive a unique account identifier and, depending on the choices you make with Google/Apple, your display name and email address. Sign in with Apple lets you hide your real email address using Apple's private relay.

2. Data We Store in the Cloud

Once signed in, the following information is stored in our database (Google Firebase), linked to your account identifier:

  • Nickname and (optionally) country
  • Game progress: best score, total score, games played, level, XP
  • In-game purchases, coin balance, owned items, and active theme
  • App settings (sound, haptics, language, UI theme)
  • Your in-progress game, so you can continue on another device
  • Friend codes, if you use the friends feature

We do not collect your real name, phone number, or physical address, and we do not require you to provide any of this information.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Some data (cached copies of the above, plus local-only preferences) is also stored on your device using local storage, so the App works offline and loads quickly.

4. Leaderboard & Friends

If you use the leaderboard or friends features, your nickname, country, and score become visible to other players. You may opt out by not using these features and by choosing not to set a country.

5. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob and its partner ad networks. These networks may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, subject to Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission) or other identifiers to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device's privacy settings (on iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking) or decline the tracking permission prompt shown by the App.

6. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

In-app purchases (coins, remove-ads, VIP subscription) are processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play, and managed on our behalf by RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment card details. RevenueCat receives your account identifier and purchase history to keep your entitlements in sync across devices. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the App is used and to diagnose crashes. This may include your account identifier, device model, OS version, and in-app events (such as starting a game or making a purchase). This data helps us fix bugs and improve the App.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers described above (Google Firebase, Google AdMob, RevenueCat, Apple/Google app stores) to operate the App, and only to the extent necessary for them to provide their services to us.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us using the email below.

10. Children's Privacy

Tiliq is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Security

We use industry-standard services (Google Firebase) to store your data and take reasonable measures to protect it. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
