Common Questions

I signed in but don't see my old progress. Make sure you're signed in with the same Google or Apple account you used before. Progress is tied to your account, not your device.

How do I remove ads? Open the in-game Store and purchase "Remove Ads" or subscribe to VIP.

How do I delete my account and data? Email us at the address above with your in-game player code (found in Settings) and we will delete your account data.
